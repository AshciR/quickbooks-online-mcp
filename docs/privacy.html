Privacy Policy

Application: QuickBooks Online MCP · Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the QuickBooks Online MCP application (the “Application”) handles data. The Application is a personal integration that connects to Intuit QuickBooks Online on the user’s own behalf.

1. Data We Access

With your authorization, the Application accesses data from your QuickBooks Online company through Intuit’s official API — for example customers, items, and invoices — solely to perform the actions you request. The Application accesses only your own company data, under the OAuth authorization you explicitly grant.

2. How Data Is Used

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used only to fulfill the operations you initiate (such as reading or creating records). The Application does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the requested operation.

3. Data Storage

The Application does not maintain its own copy of your QuickBooks Online business records. To stay connected, it stores OAuth access and refresh tokens in a private credential store controlled by the operator of the Application. These tokens are used only to authenticate API requests on your behalf and are not shared with any third party.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is exchanged only between the Application and Intuit’s QuickBooks Online API as needed to perform requested operations.

5. Security

Access tokens are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections and stored using access-controlled infrastructure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but reasonable measures are taken to protect credentials and data in transit.

6. Data Retention and Revocation

You may revoke the Application’s access at any time from your Intuit account’s connected-apps settings. Upon revocation, stored tokens become invalid and can be deleted from the credential store.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from them.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.
